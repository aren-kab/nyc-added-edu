Higher Education is a big part of New York City!

With over 140,000 degrees awarded in New York City in 2017 it is clear that universities and colleges have a large footprint in NYC. We hope to make your search for a college easier through this map with filtering options and a public/private flag.

Filter Explanations

Type: This is the Institutions Primary Awarded Degree Type by education level. Ex: There are many colleges that offer graduate & associate degrees but if they primarily offer Bachelors degrees they will fall under Bachelors.

Program Category: This filter is designed to help you find colleges based on your subject of interest. Broad institutions offers a variety of topics. The Niche category has institutions that have a specialty that don't fit into any other category.
