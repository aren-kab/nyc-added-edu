NYC Continuing Education

The Concept of public school is very cemented in our society but learning doesn't end at Grade 12. Our goal here is to show options and the access that adult citizens and families in New York have to further thier education.


Colleges and Universities, After School Programs and Citywide Public Computer Centers are shown here as avenues to learn more. Navigate around the map to the area of interest and use the check boxes to select the features you want to see.
